Dream Dolphin (aka Noriko) is a Japanese musician, whose work incorporates elements of trance, techno, IDM, ambient, and drum & bass. Music from Memory released a compilation of Japanese pop entitled 'Heisei No Oto (Japanese Left-Field Pop From The CD Age, 1989-1996)' (which you can read more about here) back in 2021, and this is where I first discovered Dream Dolphin's music.
